Statistics Analysis

You will send me part of your hand history from your tracking software. I will upload it in my database and run my personal filters over it, that will show me, how far off the numbers are, from the optimal numbers to beat the games you are playing in.
This quick oversight will give you a first idea, where probably the biggest leaks are and which topics to tackle first, so you can grow your EV.
A great tool to see where you stand, without having to dig deep inside single hands and it helps me a lot to give me an understanding, where your skill level is at and how to move on from there.

1h Leakfinder

Similar to the statistics analysis you will send me part of your database and I will use filters, to spot out leaks, based on the deviation from the optimal numbers.
We will then go over this specific area of your game. I will teach you in that hour, what the strategy behind this situation looks like, so you can adapt it, for the next time you are in the same/similar spot.
It is more specific, than the analysis and gives you clear directions, what you did wrong in this occasion and brings you one step further on the endless road, to understand the reasonings behind the game and how to gain advantages over your opponents.

Deep Review

You will send me around 500 hands out of the games you played. Either the SNGs you grind, or an MTT you played.
Then we will go over the hands in depth. Looking at the preflop ranges and population tendencies, as well as the post flop plays. Thinking about GTO lines and if they are best to apply, or if we are better of, by deviating to exploit the player pool.
After this package, you will have a lot of new input and a much better understanding, where you made mistakes and which areas you have to work on the most. You will understand deeper concepts and those will help you in future games, to make better decisions and push your EV.

You need microsoft zoom for communication with a working microphone (no camera needed), as well as being familiar with how to display your statistics, using Poker Tracker / Holdem Manager or any other tracking software, that will provide me with the overview of your frequencies and win rate.
Being familiar with solvers would also not hurt and will save us time discussing game trees and other fundamentals behind solver usage.

Expectations

What do you expect from students?
I expect dedication, being open-minded, having strong fundamentals and the ability to articulate your though process.
Furthermore, being on time as well as overall professionalism is much appreciated. I also expect you to be a winner at your stakes with decent win rates as well as already being familiar with solvers and preflop ranges.

Example 1

How does coaching work? Can you explain the process step by step?
On first session, which usually takes up to an hour, we focus on database research. Your fill in the google doc with your statistics and frequencies from a minimum of 150k hands played. We discuss your tendencies and leaks, compare them to optimal and discuss exploits based on population research that I have done. At the very end, we summarise it with a plan of what you need to be focusing on in terms of studying and what concepts you should start implementing straight away. We develop two different plans: preflop (which you should be studying on your own) and postflop (which we will study together). After your leaks are identified, we schedule more sessions together if you are interested and we focus on 1 aspect of your game at the time.

Example 2

​ Can you give an example?
After DB analysis we found out, that Hero is not VPIPing enough first in from SB, not 3betting enough from the blinds and under defending the BB vs LP openraise. Preflop plan would include studying PF ranges and making sure that after a while we are getting closer to correct frequencies. Postflop DB analysis showed, that Hero is cbetting too often OOP as PFA, not check raising enough both from BB vs LP cbet, as well as when being PFA. We plan our sessions for next couple weeks. Session 1 will be theory behind check raising from BB. This session is done as a power point presentation and GTO+ sims are used to showcase examples. After that, session 2 will be about reviewing hands that Hero played and had opportunity to check raise the flop as BB. We discuss and try to implement new knowledge and double check with postflop simulations. Hero is also adviced to check his check raise freq after addional 100k hands played and see if that freq improved and is getting closer to optimal. The same model is used to conquer cbetting OOP.
